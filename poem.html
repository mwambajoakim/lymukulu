Marching on that day,
Unbeknownst to me there she lay,
Knitting not but cooking,
Under her nose a smile, me looking,

Lately she comes on to dreams,
Yelling not but whisperings in my ears,
"Don't worry we'll soon watch those films",
Indescribable peace that calms my fear,
All for what? Maybe good things, top tier.

In short you are a beautiful, amazing person who is attractive in the way she cares
and moves. Someone who I can reason with and I am sure there is a lot more beauty I still do not know about.
